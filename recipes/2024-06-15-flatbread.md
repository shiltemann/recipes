image: recipes/images/dough1.jpg
recipe:
  person: Helena
  servings: 5 breads
  prep: 15 minutes
  ingredients:
    - 1.5 cups flour
    - 2 tsp bakpoeder
    - pinch salt
    - 3/4ths cup yoghurt
    - 1 tbsp oil

  directions: |
    1. Knead until a soft dough
    2. Add flour if needed
    3. Divide and roll
    4. Fry with a tiny bit of butter on each side

---

Soft and tasty, fold very well for e.g. halloumi broodtjes
